Learn More About Our Quiz App

Welcome to our Quiz App! This application is designed to help users test their knowledge on various subjects. Our platform allows users to log in, take quizzes, and track their scores.

Quiz Features:

  • The quiz consist of 8 questions
  • Every correct answer scores 1 point
  • Every wrong answer scores 0 point
  • Score will be calculated through the quiz
  • You have 180 seconds to finish your quiz
